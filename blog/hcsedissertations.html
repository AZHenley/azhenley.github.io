List of human-centered software engineering dissertations

11/22/2020

This is a list of dissertations that focus on the human aspects of software engineering. I really wanted a resource like this when I was a graduate student, so here it is. If you don't know where to begin, take a look at my quick start guide to research on human factors of software engineering.

Feel free to contribute to this list on GitHub or by emailing me (azh321@gmail.com).
